Acquisition criteria

What I look for in an acquisition

Most acquisitions I help with fall inside these parameters. Outside them is not a no, just a longer conversation.

Revenue range
$1M to $25M
Owner-operator scale through to lower mid-market.
Profitability
Established earnings
Stable or growing cash flow over the last three years.
Operating history
Five years or more
A track record, not a turnaround project.
Customer base
Diversified revenue
No single client greater than 40 percent of revenue.
Geography
BC and Western Canada
Strongest network in the Lower Mainland, Okanagan, and Island.
Industries
Essential operating businesses
Healthcare, logistics, trades, manufacturing, hospitality.
