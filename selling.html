How it works

The process, step by step

A structured, confidential approach from the first call through to the final close.

Step 1

Business assessment

A confidential review of your financials, operations, and market position. You leave the first call with a realistic sense of what the business is worth and how a sale would unfold.

Step 2

Positioning & buyer search

We frame the business for the right buyer profile and identify candidates through a private network. No public listings, no competitor visibility.

Step 3

Negotiation & offers

I manage the offer process, due-diligence support, and transaction documents. You see every meaningful decision and I do the negotiating on your behalf.

Step 4

Close & transition

Legal coordination, condition removal, and a transition plan that protects you after the handover. The relationship doesn’t end at close.

Is your business ready

Signals that a sale is likely to go smoothly

These are not hard filters. They are the conditions that make a sale faster, cleaner, and higher-value when they are in place.

Revenue
$1M or more in annual revenue
Operations
Three or more years of stable or growing cash flow
Team
Business can operate without the owner day-to-day
Documentation
Clean financials for the last two to three years
Motivation
Clear reason for selling (retirement, new venture, health, partnership change)
Timing
Ready to engage in the next three to twelve months

Don’t meet all of these? Reach out anyway. Context matters more than checklists, and many of the best sales started outside the standard profile.
