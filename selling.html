The transaction process

A structured approach, seven stages

A professional process designed to protect your interests, maintain confidentiality, and drive a successful outcome at every stage.

Stage 1

Consultation

A confidential review of your goals, your business profile, and what a successful exit looks like for you.

Stage 2

Valuation

A defensible market value, positioning analysis, and pricing strategy tailored to your situation.

Stage 3

Marketing

Professional information materials. Outreach to qualified buyers through a private network. No public listings.

Stage 4

Negotiation

Offer review and structured negotiation on your behalf, with you involved in every meaningful decision.

Stage 5

Due diligence

Financial, legal, and operational investigation. Coordinated information flow that protects sensitive details until the right time.

Stage 6

Documentation

Transaction agreements drafted with your lawyer. Conditions satisfied, financing finalized, dates aligned.

Stage 7

Closing

A successful transfer of ownership, with a transition plan that protects you after handover.

Preparing for a successful exit

Four readiness pillars

The most successful business sales are the result of planning well in advance. Owners who prepare thoroughly consistently achieve better outcomes: higher valuations, smoother transactions, fewer surprises.

01

Financial readiness

Clean, well-organized financial statements. Consistent revenue and demonstrated profitability across the trailing years.

02

Operational stability

Documented systems and operating procedures. Reduced owner dependency, meaning the business runs without you involved day to day.

03

Legal & structural readiness

Corporate records, contracts, and licences organized. Proper documentation in place and up to date for buyer due diligence.

04

Market positioning

A clear and credible growth story. Strong customer relationships and retention. Competitive position that supports valuation.
