About Ruchika

Boutique Advisory.
Measurable Results.

Ruchika Tekkeveetil has spent over a decade building relationships at the intersection of business and real estate in British Columbia. Where large brokerages treat transactions as volume, Ruchika treats each engagement as the singular, high-stakes moment it is for the business owner.

Her approach begins with listening — understanding what a business means to its owner, what a successful outcome truly looks like, and what timeline and terms make sense. Only then does the work begin.
