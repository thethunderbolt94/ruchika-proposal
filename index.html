About Ruchika

About Ruchika

Ruchika Tekkeveetil has spent over a decade at the intersection of business and real estate in British Columbia. She works with a small, focused roster of owners — sellers preparing for an exit, buyers searching for the right opportunity, and operators who need a defensible valuation of what they’ve built.

Her approach starts with listening: understanding what a business means to its owner, what a successful outcome actually looks like, and what timeline and terms make sense. Only then does the work begin.
